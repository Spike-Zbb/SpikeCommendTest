Description: 

A simple yet flavorful Italian pasta dish with garlic, olive oil, and red pepper flakes, perfect for a quick and satisfying dinner.

Ingredients:

1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
4 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
Salt to taste
Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions:

Cook the Spaghetti: Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water before draining.
Prepare the Sauce: While the pasta cooks, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the sliced garlic and red pepper flakes. Saut√© until the garlic is golden brown and fragrant, but not burned (about 2 minutes).
Combine Pasta and Sauce: Add the drained spaghetti to the skillet and toss to coat in the garlic oil. Add a little reserved pasta water if needed to help the sauce coat the pasta.
Finish and Serve: Stir in the chopped parsley and season with salt to taste. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

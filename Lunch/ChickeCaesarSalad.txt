Description: 

A classic Caesar salad with grilled chicken, crisp romaine lettuce, croutons, and a creamy Caesar dressing.

Ingredients:

2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
4 cups romaine lettuce, chopped
1 cup croutons
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
1/4 cup Caesar dressing (store-bought or homemade)
Olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste
1 teaspoon dried oregano (optional)

Instructions:

Cook the Chicken: Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and dried oregano if using. Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat with a drizzle of olive oil. Cook the chicken for about 6-7 minutes per side, or until fully cooked. Let rest for a few minutes before slicing.
Prepare the Salad: In a large bowl, toss the chopped romaine lettuce with Caesar dressing until evenly coated.
Assemble the Salad: Top the lettuce with sliced grilled chicken, croutons, and grated Parmesan cheese.
Serve: Serve immediately, or chill in the refrigerator until ready to eat.
